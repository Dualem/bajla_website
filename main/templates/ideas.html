First Idea

The first idea was to create an artistic recreation centre-theatre, dance studios, galleries, exhibitions. It is an autumn from the activities that breathe life into the building. The inspiration to choose the shape of the building was a shell. The entertainment centre is located on a water reservoir, which, although it is a lake, can collide with the shells. The shells can be used in several ways: *as something ancient, they often appear reflected or sunk in fossils that we can see at exhibitions, *water, vacation-miles, warm memories, wanting to return, *a shelter, a home for the organisms living in them-a feeling of comfort, safety.

Second Idea

Over time, the mass of the building changed a lot, but it was still in shell-context. The type of centre has also slightly changed. It has evolved from a typically artistic to a mixed one, by adding a swimming pool as an addition for sports fans.

Third Idea

The third evolution of the building has not changed much in terms of building mass. A big change has taken place on the inside. Another mezzanine floor was added, which was supposed to be a restaurant floor. The other two floors have undergone a complete change and some of the solutions have become final.
